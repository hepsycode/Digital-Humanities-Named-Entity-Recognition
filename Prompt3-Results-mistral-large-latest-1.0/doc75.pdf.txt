**Breve Regesto del Documento:**
Columbus and his family swore an oath in Genoa in 1470. The weavers' guild established regulations for payments from wool merchants. Antonio Colombo apprenticed his son to Leonardo Varazzino for silk weaving training. Susanna, wife of Domenico Colombo, renounced her rights to certain lands and possessions due to her dowry and dower rights.
